BADMINTON

District Meet Palaro 2023: Badminton Elementary - CHAMPION

A huge round of applause to our Badminton Team for clinching the championship in both the Boys' and Girls' Doubles categories! Rodulfo Dela Peña and Steve Dimapasok showcased incredible synergy to dominate the Boys' Doubles, while Sofia Santuyo and Martina Ambito delivered a stellar performance in the Girls' Doubles. Your dedication, teamwork, and determination have brought pride to our school. Congratulations on your well-deserved victory! 🏆✨

TABLE TENNIS

District Meet Palaro 2023: Table Tennis Elementary - CHAMPIONS

Congratulations to our Table Tennis team for an outstanding performance, earning the championship title in multiple categories! Arch Jiu Ebuenga dominated Singles B (Boys), while Ricardo Alias and Christian Angelo Gahut secured victory in Boys’ Doubles. In the Girls' events, Hanna Malota triumphed in Singles B, with Kathlyn Embile and Felicity Faith Bermudez showcasing brilliant teamwork in Girls’ Doubles. Kudos to Coaches Mr. Ian Sayson and Mr. Mel Manese for their exceptional guidance. Your hard work and success inspire us all! 🎉👏
